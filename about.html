About NLRC

Discover our vision, our mission, and what drives us. Meet Stevea Smith, a proud 24 year retired Army veteran and founder of Next Level Recreation Center (NLRC).

flag Mission

Empower veterans, youth, and community members through sports, mentorship, and wellness programs in a safe, inclusive indoor environment.

visibility Vision

To become the regional hub for veteran camaraderie, youth development, and community engagement through recreation and physical fitness.

info Snapshot

Next Level Recreation Center will be a 26,000 sq. ft. indoor recreational facility with multi-use courts, veteran-mentorship programming, youth development, leagues, camps, and community events.

The facility targets financial sustainability by Year 3 through memberships, program fees, rentals, concessions, sponsorships, and grants.
